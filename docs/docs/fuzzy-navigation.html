Find the "Find page" Button

The "Find page..." button sits at the top of the navigation sidebar, visible on every page. It appears in both the global sidebar (shown on dashboard and general pages) and the community sidebar (shown when browsing a community).

The button also displays the keyboard shortcut—Ctrl+K on Windows/Linux or ⌘K on Mac—so you can memorize it for even faster access. On mobile, the shortcut badge is hidden to save space.

Global sidebar — "Find page..." at the top of the navigation
Community sidebar — "Find page..." below the community switcher
2

Opening the Spotlight

Click the "Find page..." button or press Ctrl+K (⌘K on Mac) anywhere in the application. A search overlay appears in the center of the screen with a text input and a list of all available pages grouped by category:

  • General — Home, Dashboard, Feed
  • Browse — Browse Characters, Galleries, Media
  • My Content — My Characters, Galleries, Media
  • Liked — Liked Characters, Galleries, Media
  • Create — Create Character, Gallery, Media
  • Account — Profile, Settings
Tip: You can press Escape or click outside the overlay to close it at any time.
The spotlight overlay listing all available pages, grouped by category
3

Filtering with Fuzzy Search

Start typing and the list narrows down instantly. The search uses fuzzy matching, so you don't need to type the full page name—just a few characters is enough. Matched portions are highlighted in the results so you can see exactly what matched.

For example, typing "char" filters the list to show pages related to characters: Browse Characters, My Characters, Liked Characters, and Create Character.

You can also search by community name. Each community you belong to has its own group of pages in the spotlight—typing your community's name will surface all of its pages at once (Overview, Members, Species Management, Admin Dashboard, etc.). This is one of the fastest ways to jump between community sections.

Tip: Use the ↑ and ↓ arrow keys to move between results, then press Enter to navigate to the selected page.
Typing "char" filters results to character-related pages, with matched text highlighted
4

Navigating to a Page

Once you see the page you want, navigate to it in one of two ways:

  • Click the result directly with your mouse
  • Keyboard: use ↑/↓ to highlight the result, then press Enter

The spotlight closes automatically and you're taken to the selected page. The whole flow—from pressing Ctrl+K to landing on a new page—takes just a few keystrokes.

Keyboard-only workflow: Ctrl+K → type a few letters → Enter. You never need to leave the keyboard.
